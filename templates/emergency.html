First Aid Guide
Step-by-step emergency instructions
1
Call 108 immediately — do not wait. Tell them it's a possible heart attack.
2
Make the person sit or lie in a comfortable position. Loosen tight clothing.
3
Give aspirin (325mg) if available and person is not allergic. Ask them to chew it.
4
If unconscious and not breathing, start CPR — 30 chest compressions, 2 rescue breaths.
5
Stay calm and keep the person calm. Do not leave them alone until help arrives.

⚠️ Warning Signs — Call 108 Now

💔
Chest Pain / Pressure
Squeezing or pressure in chest, possibly spreading to arm or jaw
🧠
Sudden Weakness / Numbness
Face drooping, arm weakness, slurred speech — signs of stroke
😮‍💨
Severe Breathing Difficulty
Cannot breathe, lips turning blue — immediate emergency
💫
Sudden Dizziness / Fainting
Loss of consciousness or severe dizziness with chest pain
🩸
Very High / Low Blood Sugar
Shaking, sweating, confusion — diabetic emergency

✅ Emergency Do's & Don'ts

✅ DO These
✓ Call 108 first thing
✓ Keep the person calm and still
✓ Loosen tight clothing
✓ Note time symptoms started
✓ Stay with the person always
✓ Do CPR if trained and needed
❌ DON'T Do These
✗ Don't leave them alone
✗ Don't give food or water
✗ Don't drive yourself to hospital
✗ Don't wait to see if it gets better
✗ Don't give medicine without knowing
✗ Don't panic — stay focused
